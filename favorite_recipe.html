Julia's Garlic Fried Chicken

Julia gets her recipe from her great grandmother. It was her specialty and a customer favorite at her well known restaurant, Mama Chica's.

Ingredients Needed:

  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 cup seasoned bread crumbs
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 cup of oil for frying
  • 1 egg
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

How to cook:

  1. Mix together the garlic powder, pepper, salt, paprika, bread crumbs and flour. In a separate dish, whisk together the milk and egg.
  2. Heat the oil in an electric skillet set to 350°F (175°C). Dip the chicken into the egg and milk, then dredge in the dry ingredients until evenly coated.
  3. Fry chicken in the hot oil for about 5 minutes per side, or until the chicken is cooked through and juices run clear. Remove from the oil with a slotted spatula, and serve.
